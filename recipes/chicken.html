Juicy and Delectable Chicken Breast

Description

Are you looking for the juiciest, easiest chicken breast you can make? Then you've come to the right place. Below is our top recipe for chicken breast that anyone can make, no matter their skill level!

Ingredients

  • 1 large chicken breast
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425F
  2. Pound out chicken breast to .8 inch thickness
  3. Cover chicken in olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper
  4. Rub garlic powder, onion powder, and italian seasoning all over chicken and place on sheet
  5. Cook for 30 minutes or until chicken has internal temperature of 165F
  6. Let sit for 5 minutes
