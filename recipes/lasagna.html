Lasagna

Description

Scrumptions yet simple, lasagna is a standard meal in many American and Italian households. And it's no wonder why! There's very few people who are dissatisfied with the wonders of lasagna

Add meat, subtract meat, this dish is friendly to all types of eaters. It'll even satisfy the picky-eaters in your life!

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese

    • Tomato Sauce

  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 pound Italian sausage
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 36 ounces pasta sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

    • Cheese Mixture

  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsely
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350F and cook pasta
  2. Cook beef, sausage, onion, and garlic over medium high heat. Drain fat
  3. Stir in sauce, tomato paste, Italian season, and then simmer for five minutes
  4. Make cheese mixture
  5. Add 1 cup meat sauce to 9X13 pan and top with 3 lasagna noodles, 1/3 cheese mixture, and 1 cup of meat sauce. Repeat
  6. Cover with foil and bake 45 minutes
  7. Remove from oven and let rest for 10-15 minutes
